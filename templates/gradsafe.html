Problem Statement

Many educational institutions struggle to identify at-risk students early enough to provide timely interventions. Existing systems often rely on manual analysis of student performance, which is time-consuming and prone to errors. GradSafe.io addresses this by providing real-time analysis of student performance to predict GPA and detention risks, enabling proactive support for students.

Screenshots

Detailed Description

GradSafe is a data-driven student performance monitoring and dropout risk prediction platform. It uses machine learning models to forecast student's final grades and assess the probability of dropout based on various academic, behavioral, and socio-economic factors. Designed as a web-based Single Page Application (SPA), GradSafe allows schools and educators to proactively identify at-risk students and take preventive action.
